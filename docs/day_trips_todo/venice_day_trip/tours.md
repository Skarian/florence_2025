## Doge's Palace Hidden Treasures / Secret Itineraries (official)

Official tour page:
- <a href="https://palazzoducale.visitmuve.it/en/visita/secret-itineraries-tour/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://palazzoducale.visitmuve.it/en/visita/secret-itineraries-tour/</a>

Key details (official):
- Guided tour duration is about 1 hour 15 minutes.
- Tours are scheduled at fixed times (check the official page for the current schedule).
- Ticket price: EUR 40 (adult), EUR 20 (ages 6-14), free under 6.
- Booking is handled by phone or email (no online booking for these tours).

Booking notes:
- This ticket includes Doge's Palace plus entry to other St Mark's Square museums (Correr, Archaeological Museum, Monumental Rooms of the Biblioteca Marciana).
- Only add if it fits your train schedule and you still plan to keep the day flexible.
