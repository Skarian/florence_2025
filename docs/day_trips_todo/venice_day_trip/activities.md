## Required: Gondola ride

Official pricing and rules (Comune di Venezia):
- Day rate (9:00 AM-7:00 PM): EUR 90 for 30 minutes.
- Night rate (7:00 PM-4:00 AM): EUR 110 for 35 minutes.
- Max 5 passengers per gondola.
- Ride length can vary slightly due to traffic, tide, and weather; longer rides are priced proportionally.

Official guidance and booking:
- Rates are fixed citywide. You can book directly with a gondola "stazio" (stand) using the official contact list.
- If you prefer not to pre-book, you can queue at a stazio, but availability is not guaranteed.
