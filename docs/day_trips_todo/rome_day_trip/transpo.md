Fare flexibility (decision aid, official sources):
- Trenitalia Base: refundable before departure with a 20% deduction; changes allowed. Economy: one time change allowed, no refunds. Optional tiRimborso can add a refund option to some discounted fares.
- Italo Economy: unlimited changes up to 3 minutes before departure with a 20% fee plus fare difference; refunds are 40%.
- Italo Flex: unlimited changes up to 3 minutes before departure for free; refunds are 80%.
- Italo Refund Option: paid add-on allowing an 80% refund if you cancel up to 72 hours before departure.
- Italo Extra Time: missed-train rebooking within 1 hour for a EUR 10 fee plus any fare difference.
- Italo Leave Now: travel up to 2 hours earlier on the day of travel for a EUR 12 fee.
